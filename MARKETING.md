Take control of your mental wellness with **Puff** - a free, ad-free breathing exercise app designed to help you manage stress, improve focus, and promote relaxation.

## Why Use Puff?

- **Stress Relief:** Scientifically-backed breathing patterns that activate your parasympathetic nervous system
- **Anxiety Management:** Quick 1-minute sessions for moments of panic or overwhelm
- **Better Sleep:** Evening wind-down routines to help you drift off peacefully
- **Focus Enhancement:** Box breathing techniques to sharpen concentration before work or study

## How It Works

Puff guides you through simple breathing exercises using an intuitive visual interface. The adorable cloud mascot breathes along with you, creating a calming companion for your wellness journey.

### Features

- Multiple breathing patterns (4-7-8, box breathing, alternate nostril)
- Customizable session durations
- Progress tracking and history
- Zero ads, zero subscriptions - completely free forever
- Works offline once downloaded

## Perfect For

- People looking to improve sleep quality
- Mindfulness beginners

## About Puff the Mascot

Puff is a CSS-only animated cloud that breathes along with you during each breathing session. The mascot changes color based on the breathing phase:

- **Blue** (inhale): Represents drawing in fresh air, calming energy
- **Pink** (exhale): Represents releasing tension and stress
- **Light blue** (hold/idle): Neutral state between phases

Puff's gentle animation creates a soothing visual rhythm that helps users stay present with their breathing practice.
